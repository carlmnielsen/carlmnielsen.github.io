Enhancing Ecommerce Checkout - Kennards Hire

Summary

I was part of a project team that optimised the Kennards Hire ecomm cart process by implementing a streamlined stepper interface. Through wireframing, protoyping and user testing, I aimed to enhance user flow, reduce friction points, and drive higher conversion rates. The result was a significant improvement in customer satisfaction and increased business success.

Problem

Through regular conversion funnel monitoring, it became apparent that the cart had become a bottleneck for users continuing through to checkout.

Research

Analysis of user recordings, heatmaps and tracked events revealed that the order of hire details presented in cart was creating confusion for users. Overall content and visual hierarchy, and absense of consistent status messaging also constributed to user disorientation.

UI wireframing and prototyping

Based off the qualitative and quantitative data gathered, medium fidelity wireframes and a prototype were developed for user testing.

Testing

Surveys and un-moderated testing were performed to measure the intuitiveness and user understanding of the prototyped stepper, and overall user satisfaction.
